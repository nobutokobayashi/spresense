283 /* DMA Channel mapping. Each DMA channel has a mapping to one of several
284  * possible sources/sinks of data. The requests from peripherals assigned to a
285  * channel are multiplexed together before entering the DMA block. This means
286  * that only one request on a given channel can be enabled at once.
287  *
288  * Alternative DMA channel selections are provided with a numeric suffix like _1,
289  * _2, etc. Drivers, however, will use the pin selection without the numeric suffix.
290  * Additional definitions are required in the board.h file.
291  */

56 /* LED definitions ******************************************************************/
57 /* The pcDuino v1 has four green LEDs; three can be controlled from software. Two
58  * are tied to ground and, hence, illuminated by driving the output pins to a high
59  * value:
60  *
61  * 1. LED1 SPI0_CLK SPI0_CLK/UART5_RX/EINT23/PI11
62  * 2. LED5 IPSOUT From the PMU (not controllable by software)
63  *
64  * And two are pull high and, hence, illuminated by grounding the output:
65  *
66  * 3. LED3 RX_LED LCD1_D16/ATAD12/KP_IN6/SMC_DET/EINT16/CSI1_D16/PH16
67  * 4. LED4 TX_LED LCD1_D15/ATAD11/KP_IN5/SMC_VPPPP/EINT15/CSI1_D15/PH15
68  */

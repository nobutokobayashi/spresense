55 /* NXP/Freescale has familes and technology generations (sometimes seen as processor
56  * speed). These are organized into feature families, and faster speeds sometimes
57  * have extended features. Families are K02 K10 K20 K22 K24 K30 K40 K50 K60 K64 K65
58  * K66 K70 K80
59  *
60  * So far only two variations/generations on the RTC have been discovered.
61  * GEN1 RTC_TSR TPR TAR TCR CR SR LR IER WAR RAR
62  * GEN2 RTC_TSR TPR TAR TCR CR SR LR IER TTSR MER MCLR MCHR WAR RAR
63  *
64  * KINETIS RTC_GEN1 K20P32-->K20P81M K22 K40 K50 K60@100Mhz K64 120MHz
65  * Assumed K10 K11
66  *
67  * KINETIS_RTC_GEN2 K02 K20P144M K26P169 K60@120Mhz K65x K66x
68  *
69  * Note current naming doesn't allow GEN1:MK60FN...Q10 & GEN2:MK60FN...Q12
70  */
